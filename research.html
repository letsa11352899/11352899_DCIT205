Get To Know US

Things get interesting because you are eager to know more , or you become eager to know more because it is interesting?

Organisation
The University of Ghana is one of the best public universities in Africa. We have a significant impact on people, the planet, and our region. Whether we are introducing youth to an endless future or using fearless research to take on the great concerns of our day. Approximately 2.64% of the more over 38,000 students that UG teaches each year are majors in computer science. We change the world around us by transforming ideas into action. Go to our news website, UG News, to learn more about our influence.
UG-CSCD People
If a significant challenge motivates you, you've come to the correct spot! The University of Ghana's computer science community exhorts its members to be bold and adventurous in embracing new challenges that push boundaries beyond their wildest dreams.
Distance Learning
From anywhere, experience a UG-CDCD classroom. With a variety of online delivery options, you may personalize your degree to meet your schedule and benefit from in-person interactions with professors and real-time lectures. Our virtual classroom upholds the same standard of academic excellence while offering students additional learning opportunities.
Building Our Future
What then characterizes our faculty, students, and community members? It is, above all, our unwavering optimism and our faith in the endless possibilities. It's a link to people both nearby and far away. a thirst for socioeconomic change that drives us to overcome obstacles and strive for advancement. It's the belief that by working together, we can make the world a better place. Come along on the ride.
